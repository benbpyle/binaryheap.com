Hey — I'm Benjamen Pyle, a developer who enjoys building things for the web. This site is where I write about software engineering, cloud architecture, serverless, and whatever else I'm tinkering with.

## What I do

I spend most of my time working with TypeScript, Rust, Go, modern frameworks, and cloud infrastructure on AWS. I care about performance, clean code, and shipping things that actually work well for the people who use them.

## Projects

**Homeschool Ace** — An edtech platform I'm building to help homeschool families manage curriculum, track progress, and stay organized.
